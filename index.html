Mother Road Brewing Dashboard

A private web-based dashboard for Mother Road Brewing Company. The brewery relies on multiple data sources for executive and management teams to create production forecasts, sales goals, and monitor financial performance. The dashboard aggregates data from applications such as VIP and Square in order to present them to employees in a more effective way. Current sales data can be tracked and compared with historical data, and employees are provided with an intra-office communication platform.
